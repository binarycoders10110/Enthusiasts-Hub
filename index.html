Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, simply click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill out the required information in the registration form, and once completed, you'll receive a confirmation email. Follow the link in the email to activate your account, and you'll be a member with full access to our features.

As a member, you'll enjoy exclusive access to a range of features, including PDF study files, event information, board passer pictures, interactive games, and various utilities. Membership also grants you participation in our community forums, where you can connect with other members, share insights, and stay updated on the latest developments.

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and you'll receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password. Follow the provided link to set a new password and regain access to your account. If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
